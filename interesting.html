My interest in IT and ideal job in an IT field.

My interest in IT was sparked from a young age as I grew up around computers and always enjoyed working with them. Learning how to fix problems with my computers and editing game files was the first experience I had with troubleshooting and toying with a computer. I chose to study at RMIT as I feel that they are a leader in the field of IT and provide good resources and learning experiences for future endeavours relating to IT. They provide hands-on practical work that will most likely become applicable in future jobs. In my studies at RMIT I am expecting to learn the skills necessary to enter the field of IT with a great understanding of the types of jobs available and what I would most enjoy doing in and IT field.

The ideal job for me in an IT field is most likely work as an IT project manager.

The current skills I have for this position are lacking, as currently I only have the communication skills and experience with the windows OS and Microsoft Office.
